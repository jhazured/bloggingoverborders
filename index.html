I was born in the Phillipines, raised in Thailand and currently live in Perth, Western Australia.

My parents are from Hong Kong and Switzerland and I spent most of my childhood travelling through different countries. I went to primary school in a small town around 6 hours north of Perth with a population of approximately 2000 people.

It's no wonder the travel bug has bit me and encouraged me to go visit new and existing places!

My interests include travel and photography, and I am currently studying IT at Curtin University.

I wish to share some of my experiences with other people who share my passion for travel. I hope you enjoy my blog!
